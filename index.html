I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Michigan (UofM). I am advised by Dr. Todd Hollon and Dr. Honglak Lee. My current research is about language-image pre-training, visual instruction tuning, and one-shot segmentation for medical imaging. I hold an M.S. in Electronic and Computer Engineering from the UofM, during which I was advised by Dr. Liyue Shen, and a B.S. in Electronic Engineering from Nanjing University.
